(General/Operation Manager)


The career that I see myself doing in the future is to work in business. I chose this career because I always found business very interesting and I wish to own my own business one day.

Some of the responsibilities of a General/Operations Manager are the following

  • Oversee daily operations
  • Enforce policies
  • Oversee facilities
  • Develop budgets
The average salary for a General/Operations Manager is $123,030 per year, ranging anywhere from $65,660 - $218,550. In order to work as a General/Operations Manager, you need at least a Bachelors degree.
